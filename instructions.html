How to Use Your Creative Works Scrapbook App

Welcome to your creative space! This guide will help you get started with building pages and organizing them into different scrapbooks.

1. Managing Your Scrapbooks

The "My Scrapbooks" section on the left sidebar is your main hub for organizing projects. A scrapbook is simply a collection of pages.

  • Create a New Scrapbook: To start a new project, click the Create New Scrapbook button. A prompt will appear asking for a name.
  • Select a Scrapbook: The currently selected scrapbook is highlighted with a blue border. All new pages you save will be added to this specific scrapbook. Simply click on a scrapbook's name to make it the active one.
  • Delete a Scrapbook: You can delete a scrapbook and all of its pages by clicking the "Delete" button next to its name. Note that you cannot delete the last remaining scrapbook.

2. Building a Page

The "Work Area" in the center is where you build your pages. The "Graphics Library" on the right provides the images you can use.

  • Drag Images: To add an image to your page, simply click on an image in the "Graphics Library" and drag it over to the "Work Area".
  • Adjusting Images: Once an image is in the work area, you can click and drag it to move it. You can also resize it by dragging the corners.
  • Special Effects: Before dragging an image, you can check the "Header" or "Transparent" boxes to apply special effects.
    • Header: This makes the image appear on top of other images.
    • Transparent: This makes the image semi-transparent.
  • Delete an Image: To remove an image from the work area, hold down the Alt key on your keyboard and click on the image.
  • Page Title: Use the input box at the top of the "Work Area" to give your page a unique title before saving.

3. Saving Your Work

Saving a page adds a snapshot of your "Work Area" to your selected scrapbook.

  • Saving a Page: Once your page is complete, click the Save Page button. A thumbnail of your page will appear in the "Pages" list under your current scrapbook.
  • Saving to the Correct Scrapbook: The page will always be saved to the scrapbook that is currently highlighted in the "My Scrapbooks" list.
  • Accessing Saved Pages: The "Pages" list displays all the pages within your currently selected scrapbook. You can scroll through this list to see all of your saved work.
  • Loading a Saved Page: Click on any page thumbnail in the list to load that page back into the "Work Area" for editing.
  • Deleting a Saved Page: To delete a page, click the "Delete" button on its thumbnail.
Tip: The app saves everything locally, so your work will be here the next time you open the page!
